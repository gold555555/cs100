Black Ivory Coffee is special not only for its taste, but also for its rarity, which makes a symbol of luxury. Producing this coffee requires up to 33 kilograms of coffee cherries to yield just 1 kilogram of roasted coffee, since many beans are damaged or lost during the elephant’s digestive process. Each year, only about 225 kilograms are produced, making Black Ivory Coffee one of the rarest coffees in the world.
